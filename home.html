Furthermore, I have 10+ years of experience in the field of Architecture Design and Construction Area as a supervisor. So far, I have made my own projects as well with high quality and full dedication to them. If you love what you do, there are no complaints.

However, given the fact that I'm a normal human being and I'm one of the "time is gold" type of guy, I'm also inside the coding world as a Developer Web Designer. Therefore, I'm improving daily conquering different ways to code and debug in no time. Making a FullStack Bootcamp made me realize that it's a real game changer and keeps my mind busy 24/7 applying these skills within my architectural responsibilities and mindset. There are no restrictions when making the time worthwhile is an option, so I love every moment of it.

My goals in the short term are always to keep evolving and adapting to the world that we live in, so I'm trying to apply that mechanism in Architecture Design and Full Stack Web Development. Not least, my main objectives are to achieve greatness in both areas, working for a big company as a developer to apply those skills and all my knowledge to help in every aspect I can, so that I can apply my architectural skills to my personal journey as an Architect as well.

At last, setting the professional side apart, I'm definitely the type of person that loves extreme activities. I will rather be in cold weather than hot. Thus, it's not weird that my main hobby is snowboarding and skydiving. I love sports, I'm a cinephile, and I do love spending my time in coffee shops with my laptop and a book from time to time.

I'm looking forward to seeing what's next and enjoying the path of it.
